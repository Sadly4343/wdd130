The purpose for our rafting company was developed through arduous struggles we faced in the great rafting depression of 1956. However through our strength and perseverance of our owner Reginald Bacon the 3rd we were able to overcome those tough times. The thing we strive for now is to create a positive and enjoyable experience for future adventureres such as yourself. You will be in for the most thrilling experience of your life so get prepared and look into our trip section for further information.

We are the leading provider in all things rafting. This might seem like an ego but we strive to provide you with the safest and most enjoyable experience and promise our equipment and floats are the strongest and will keep you safe while you enjoy the thrilling adventure that is rafting.
